Who we are

About Us

Hi, I'm Loops ✨

Handmade by Loops is a small, one-woman crochet studio based in Pakistan. What started as a quiet hobby during online classes grew into something much more beautiful — a business built on creativity, patience, and love.

I began crocheting in 2021 after watching a YouTube tutorial late at night. Within a week, I was completely hooked (pun intended). I started gifting my creations to friends and family, and the response was overwhelming. People wanted more — and slowly, Handmade by Loops was born.

Every item in this shop is made by hand, one stitch at a time. I don't use machines. I don't outsource. Each product takes hours — sometimes days — to complete, and that's exactly how I want it to be. Because when you hold something handmade, you can feel the time and care that went into it.

This is slow craft. This is intentional making. And it's something I'm incredibly proud of.

We recommend hand washing in cool water with mild soap. Avoid wringing — gently squeeze and lay flat to dry away from direct sunlight.
